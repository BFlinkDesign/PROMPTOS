# Adversarial self-review (before shipping)

> Review the artifact you just produced as a hostile senior reviewer who
> did not build it. For each claim: where is the evidence, does the cited
> source actually say that, is the number recomputed or transcribed? List
> every internal cross-reference and verify it resolves. Find the three
> weakest claims and either harden them with a fresh source read or
> downgrade their stated trust tier. Report failures plainly.
